FAQ

Answers for procurement teams.

Still have questions? Our institutional sales team responds within one business day.

Do you accept institutional bulk orders?
Yes. We specialize in B2B bulk purchasing for hospitals, distributors, and growing clinics. We can easily scale to accommodate orders of any size.
Can products be customized with our hospital logo?
Absolutely. We offer custom embroidery for scrubs and lab coats, as well as color-coding options to differentiate hospital departments (e.g., ICU, Pediatrics, General Ward).
Do you provide formal B2B quotations for procurement committees?
Yes. Simply share your required Bill of Quantities (BOQ), and our team will provide a formal, itemized quotation for your hospital's purchasing board.
Are your products compliant with medical standards?
Yes, all surgical dressings, consumables, and hospital furniture are manufactured to meet or exceed standard medical safety and hygiene regulations. Request our compliance certificates →
What is the typical delivery timeline?
Timelines depend on order volume and customization. Standard catalog items are dispatched within 5–7 business days, while custom bulk orders receive a strict delivery schedule during the quotation phase.
